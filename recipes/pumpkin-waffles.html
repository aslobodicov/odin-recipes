Pumpkin Waffles

Description

These light and tasty pumpkin waffles are served with a homemade apple cider syrup. This recipe is a fall, winter, and special occasion family favorite.
The waffles are fairly easy to make and delicious — they're sure to please even picky eaters.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour

  • ¼ cup packed brown sugar

  • 4 teaspoons baking powder

  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

  • 1 teaspoon ground allspice

  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger

  • ½ teaspoon salt

  • 2 cups milk

  • 1 cup canned pumpkin

  • 4 eggs, separated

  • ¼ cup butter, melted
